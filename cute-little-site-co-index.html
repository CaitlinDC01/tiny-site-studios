Questions people usually ask

How long does it take?

Most simple sites can be created once all content is submitted. Timeline depends on the complexity of the request and my current availability.

What counts as an update?

Small updates include changes to wording, links, images, sections, colors, layout tweaks, or details. Major redesigns or new concepts are outside the included updates.

Do I need to know anything about websites?

Nope. You send the information, images, links, and general vibe. I handle the build.

Can you help me publish it?

Yes. I can provide the site files or help you get it published using a simple hosting option like GitHub Pages.

Can you make it match a theme?

Yes. Themes are the fun part. You can send colors, inspiration images, mood words, examples, or just tell me the vibe.

Can you update it later?

Yes. Updates are included based on your package. After that, additional updates can be purchased separately.
