FAQ

What counts as a small update?

Small updates include edits to wording, links, images, colors, layout tweaks, sections, dates, times, addresses, and other existing site details.

Do I need to know anything about websites?

Nope. You send the information, images, links, and general vibe. I handle the build.

Can you help me publish it?

Yes. I can provide the site files or help you get it published using a simple hosting option.

Can you make it match a theme?

Yes. Themes are the fun part. You can send colors, inspiration images, mood words, screenshots, or just tell me the vibe.

Can you update it later?

Yes. Updates are included based on your package. After that, additional updates can be purchased separately.
