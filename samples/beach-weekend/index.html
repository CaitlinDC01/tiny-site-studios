House Rules

  • Rinse off before coming inside.
  • Keep sand outside when possible.
  • Quiet hours after 11 PM.
  • Start a towel load before check-out.
  • Take kitchen trash to the outside bins each night.

Beach Rules

  • No glass on the beach.
  • Keep an eye on kids near the water.
  • Bring trash back to the house.
  • Use sunscreen and refill water bottles often.
  • Check the flag color before swimming.

Parking

  • Driveway fits 6 vehicles.
  • Use the side lot for overflow parking.
  • Do not block neighboring driveways.
  • Keep one space open for grocery and ice runs.

Emergency

  • Call 911 for emergencies.
  • Closest urgent care: Crystal Beach Medical Center.
  • Nearest pharmacy: Big Store Pharmacy.
  • Emergency contact: Aunt Sarah.

Before We Leave

  • Strip beds and pile towels by the laundry room.
  • Empty fridge of personal food.
  • Check under beds and in bathrooms.
  • Lock all doors and windows.
What’s Around

Useful spots nearby.

For grocery runs, rainy-day breaks, and easy extras without overplanning the weekend.

Quick Stops

  • The Big Store for groceries, ice, sunscreen, and beach basics.
  • Local bait shops for fishing supplies and last-minute gear.
  • Gas stations along Hwy 87 for drinks, snacks, and ice.

Easy Food Nearby

  • Stingaree Restaurant for seafood and bay views.
  • Tiki Beach Bar & Grill for casual burgers and baskets.
  • Jose’s Cantina for Tex-Mex if nobody wants to cook.

Things To Do

  • Walk the beach at sunrise or sunset.
  • Take the Bolivar Ferry for dolphin spotting.
  • Visit Fort Travis Seashore Park for views and photos.
  • Shop for beach souvenirs near the main strip.
