Quick FAQ

Where should I park?

Use the main lot on the west side of the venue. Overflow parking is available across the street.

Can I bring flowers or gifts?

Yes. Small gifts and flowers are welcome. There will be a card basket at dinner.

What should I wear?

Dressy casual is perfect. The family color palette is blush, cream, and navy if you want to coordinate for photos.

Will there be food?

Yes. Dinner is planned after the ceremony, and dessert is absolutely happening.
